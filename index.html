Our Story

Built on Discipline.
Defined by Results.

Special Services Protection was founded in Atlanta, Georgia with a single mission: to provide professional, reliable security services that clients can genuinely trust. Built on a foundation of military precision and corporate professionalism, we bring an elite standard to every assignment.

Our team consists of experienced security professionals — many with backgrounds in law enforcement and military service. This foundation instills the discipline, situational awareness, and decision-making skills that separate SSP from the competition.

Based in Atlanta and serving the greater Georgia area, we have built a reputation for integrity, reliability, and results. Whether protecting a Fortune 500 corporate campus or a private residence, our commitment to excellence never wavers.

The Man Behind The Standard

Dijon Lowery
Founder & CEO — Special Services Protection

Dijon Lowery founded Special Services Protection in Atlanta, Georgia with a clear vision: to build a security firm that operated at the highest level — one where professionalism, discretion, and elite execution were non-negotiable.

With a career spanning over a decade in the security industry, Dijon has personally overseen protection details at some of the most high-profile events in the country — from Super Bowl weekend to major concert tours and exclusive private functions across the Southeast.

His hands-on leadership style and direct involvement with clients sets SSP apart. When you work with Special Services Protection, you work with ownership — not a call center.
