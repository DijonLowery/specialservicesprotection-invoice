Our Story

Founded on Experience.
Built for Excellence.

Special Services Protection was founded in 2018 by Bob Lowery, who has amassed over 38 years of experience in all aspects of the security industry.

We are a premier provider of professional security solutions, specializing in personal security details and crowd management services. Our mission is to deliver discreet, reliable, and highly trained protection personnel who ensure the safety and peace of mind of our clients in any environment.

Founded in Atlanta, Georgia, Special Services Protection has earned a reputation for excellence serving high-profile individuals, corporate executives, event organizers, and public venues with unmatched vigilance and professionalism — nationwide and internationally.
