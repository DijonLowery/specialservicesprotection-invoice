Our Story

Founded on Experience.
Built for Excellence.

Special Services Protection was founded in 2018 by Bob Lowery, who has amassed over 38 years of experience in all aspects of the security industry.

Special Services Protection is a premier provider of professional security solutions, specializing in personal security details and crowd management services. Our mission is to deliver discreet, reliable, and highly trained protection personnel who ensure the safety and peace of mind of our clients in any environment.

With over a decade of experience, Special Services Protection has earned a reputation for excellence by serving high-profile individuals, corporate executives, event organizers, and public venues with unmatched vigilance and professionalism.

Founder & CEO — Special Services Protection

Bob Lowery founded Special Services Protection in 2018 after building over 38 years of hands-on experience across all aspects of the security industry. His career spans executive protection, crowd management, event security, and risk assessment at every level — from local venues to national stages.

Under Bob's leadership, Special Services Protection has become a trusted name for high-profile individuals, corporate executives, event organizers, and public venues throughout Atlanta and the greater Georgia area. His philosophy is simple: every client deserves discreet, reliable, and elite-level protection — no matter the assignment.

When you work with SSP, you work with a team built by someone who has been in the field — not behind a desk. That lived experience is the foundation of everything we do.
