# SSP Command Center Product Blueprint

## Mission

SSP Command Center is an operations-first internal platform for Special Services Protection. It helps the company find work, staff work, execute work, get paid, and turn completed work into brand momentum.

## Experience Split

- Internal Command Platform: owner, admin, and manager users.
- Associate Portal: associate self-service for profile, availability, assignments, documents, and support.

## Systems Of Record

- SSP Command owns operations, scheduling, associates, availability, CRM, outreach workflow, content workflow, AI actions, tasks, and alerts.
- Paychex owns payroll records and payroll processing.
- Gmail owns email delivery, invoice communication, outreach communication, and message threads.
- LinkedIn owns company publishing.

## Roles

- Owner: full visibility and control.
- Admin: broad operational control.
- Manager: scoped control by assigned city, event, site, or team.
- Associate: self-service only.

## Core Workflows

1. Capture leads for security opportunities.
2. Generate outreach and send through Gmail.
3. Convert won opportunities into events or contracts.
4. Collect associate profiles and availability.
5. Generate schedules based on availability, location, credentials, and workload.
6. Execute jobs and track operational risks.
7. Invoice the client and follow up through Gmail.
8. Sync payroll readiness with Paychex.
9. Generate LinkedIn job recaps and brand content.

## AI Agents

- Executive Briefing Agent: summarizes risk and next actions.
- Operations Agent: answers cross-system company questions.
- Scheduling Agent: recommends assignments and flags conflicts.
- Associate Intake Agent: helps collect and normalize associate profiles.
- Collections Agent: drafts reminders and summarizes payment risk.
- Outreach Agent: drafts targeted CRM outreach.
- Content Agent: creates LinkedIn posts and job recaps.

## AI Action Levels

- Suggest: draft or recommend only.
- One-click: user approves before sending or changing records.
- Auto-run: approved recurring workflows only.

## V1 Scope

- Authentication and role model prototype.
- Command dashboard with cross-module KPIs.
- Operational calendar.
- Associate profiles, intake links, and availability.
- Schedule generation from associate profiles.
- Invoice tracker surface with Gmail reminder workflow placeholder.
- Paychex payroll sync/review placeholder.
- Internal CRM and one-click outreach workflow placeholder.
- LinkedIn content studio and publishing queue placeholder.
- Copilot that can answer operational questions using dashboard data.
